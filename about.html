Vision

A decentralised, sustainable, and connected world where no computing is wasted and to create a global network of local cloud computing resources that are sustainable, cost-effective, and secure.

Mission

Empower people and organisations to earn and save through cheaper, accessible, and sustainable computing by utilising spare capacity.

Today's Problems, Our Solutions

Problem

Over $1 trillion is spent on IT hardware annually.

Yet, its computational resources are unused up to 50% of the time.

Solution

ProsoftChain connects blockchain developers and services to a global pool of computing power. This enables up to 10x more cost-effective computing, greener computing and access to a global network to power your next Metaverse, Dynamic NFT, AI, Machine Learning, DeFi or any computationally demanding dApps and smart contracts.
